Facilities

Welcome to our hotel! We are delighted to have you as our guest. Our hotel offers a range of exceptional facilities to enhance your stay. Enjoy our luxurious rooms, state-of-the-art fitness center, refreshing swimming pool, and exquisite dining options at our restaurant. Our dedicated staff is here to ensure your comfort and provide exceptional service throughout your stay. We hope you have a memorable experience with us.

Restaurant

Our hotel restaurant facility offers a delightful culinary experience. Enjoy a wide range of delicious local and international dishes prepared by our skilled chefs. Immerse yourself in a comfortable and elegant ambiance as our friendly staff delivers excellent service.

5AM-12PM

Spa

Our hotel's SPA facility provides a serene and rejuvenating experience. Indulge in a variety of relaxing treatments and therapies designed to pamper your mind, body, and soul. Let our expert therapists take care of you in a tranquil and luxurious environment.

10AM-10PM

Mini Market

The hotel's mini market facility offers convenience right at your doorstep. Find a wide selection of essential items and snacks to fulfill your immediate needs. Whether you need toiletries, beverages, or snacks, our mini market has got you covered.

24 Hours

Swimming Pool

The swimming pool facility on the 8th floor of the hotel provides a refreshing oasis for our guests. Enjoy a leisurely swim or soak up the sun on the poolside loungers. The pool offers breathtaking views of the surrounding cityscape, creating a serene and relaxing atmosphere. Escape the hustle and bustle of the city and unwind in our stunning rooftop swimming pool.

5AM-10PM

Gym

The hotel gym facility is a perfect place to stay fit and active during your stay. Equipped with state-of-the-art fitness equipment, you can enjoy a comprehensive workout. Our gym provides a motivating environment for guests to achieve their fitness goals.

5AM-10PM

Grand Ballroom

The grand ballroom facility in our hotel offers a stunning venue for your special events and celebrations. With its spacious layout and elegant decor, it creates a sophisticated atmosphere. Our dedicated staff ensures flawless execution to make your event truly memorable.

Coffe Shop

The coffee shop facility within the hotel provides a cozy and inviting space to enjoy your favorite brews. Offering a wide selection of coffee, tea, and delicious pastries, it's an ideal spot for a quick pick-me-up or a relaxed meeting. Our friendly baristas are ready to serve you with their expertise and ensure a delightful coffee experience.

8AM-12PM

Receptionist

Our hotel offers a 24-hour reception facility, ensuring assistance is available around the clock. Our dedicated team of receptionists is ready to assist you with any inquiries, check-ins, or check-outs at any time. Enjoy the convenience and peace of mind knowing that our reception is always open to cater to your needs.
