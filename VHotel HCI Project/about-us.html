About Us

As one of the legendary hotels in Jakarta, VhoTel has been serving guests since 1999. With rich experience and impeccable service, VhoTel is a popular choice for tourists and business visitors.

The history of VhoTel starts in 1999, when this hotel first opened. Since then, VhoTel has become an icon in the world of hospitality in Jakarta. With its strategic location, VhoTel offers easy access to various tourist attractions, shopping centers and business areas in the city.

One of the main advantages of VhoTel is the wide variety of room options that are offered to guests. Starting from rooms with 1 bedroom, 2 bedrooms to luxury suites with 3 bedrooms and a kitchen, VhoTel can accommodate various lodging needs. Each room is well-designed and equipped with modern amenities to ensure maximum comfort and convenience for guests.

Apart from that, VhoTel also provides various facilities to fulfill guest's needs. The VhoTel Spa offers a relaxing and restorative experience with a variety of rejuvenating body treatments. A gym equipped with modern equipment allows guests to stay active and keep fit while staying at VhoTel. If you want to relax and enjoy the sun, a beautiful swimming pool is also available at VhoTel.
